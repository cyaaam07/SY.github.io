Legendary

Formed in Manchester in 1982, The Smiths became one of the most influential bands of the 1980s alternative music scene. Known for their jangly guitars, raw emotion, and Morrissey's poetic lyrics, they crafted a sound that was both melancholic and rebellious. Despite their short time together, the band released four legendary studio albums, including The Queen Is Dead and Meat Is Murder. Their songs like "There Is a Light That Never Goes Out" and "This Charming Man" continue to inspire millions, with over 18 million monthly listeners on Spotify today.
